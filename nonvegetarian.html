Non-Vegetarian Recipes

Tandoori Chicken

Ingredients:
  • 2 skinless chicken legs with thighs
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt to taste
Instructions:
  1. Mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, spices, lemon juice, and salt to prepare the marinade.
  2. Marinate the chicken for at least 2 hours (preferably overnight).
  3. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and bake for 25-30 minutes, turning once halfway through.
  4. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve with a side of green chutney.

Fish Curry

Ingredients:
  • 2 fillets of white fish (e.g., pomfret or cod)
  • 1 cup coconut milk (light)
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind pulp
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish
Instructions:
  1. Heat a non-stick pan and add mustard seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add coconut milk, tamarind pulp, turmeric, chili powder, and green chilies. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Add the fish fillets and cook for 10 minutes on low heat until the fish is tender.
  4. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve with brown rice or quinoa.

Chicken Soup

Ingredients:
  • 200g chicken breast, shredded
  • 4 cups chicken stock (low sodium)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 carrot, finely chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish
Instructions:
  1. Heat a pot and sauté onion and ginger-garlic paste until fragrant.
  2. Add chicken stock, carrot, turmeric, and black pepper. Bring to a boil.
  3. Add shredded chicken and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.

Egg Curry

Ingredients:
  • 4 boiled eggs
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, pureed
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 100g paneer, cubed
  • 1/2 cup green peas (fresh or frozen)
  • Fresh coriander for garnish
  • Salt to taste
Instructions:
  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onion and ginger-garlic paste until golden brown.
  2. Add tomato puree, spices, and cook until oil separates.
  3. Add green peas and paneer cubes, and cook for a few minutes until heated through.
  4. Add boiled eggs, mix gently, and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve with millet roti or brown rice.

Prawn Masala

Ingredients:
  • 200g prawns, cleaned
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish
Instructions:
  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onion and ginger-garlic paste until golden brown.
  2. Add tomatoes, spices, and cook until the mixture thickens.
  3. Add prawns and cook for 5-7 minutes until tender.
  4. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve with steamed rice.
